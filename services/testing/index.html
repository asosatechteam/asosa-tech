TESTING & QA SERVICES

Quality Assurance is a key part of the development cycle from technical and business point of view. It is QA that makes sure your product will work as it should and will not crash or lag all of a sudden upon performing its tasks.

About Quality Assurance

If we can ask you one thing, it would be this: think beyond bugs when you hear Quality Assurance. The job of a QA engineer is much more diverse than simply looking for errors in the code. One of the main reasons we involve QA engineers in every single project that we do is because we want to be proud of the products we develop for our clients - and that means not just errorless code, but also a product that the users will love. Quality Assurance engineers aren’t just obnoxious teammates who point at bugs, but they are the ones who make sure the user’s experience is up to par.

In technical terms, the APP Solutions provides both manual testing and automation testing services as well as QA consultancy services. Automated tests do the best job while checking constantly repeating tasks (and save up to 20% of time required for testing), and manual testing gives the best feeling of the product and audit.

Elements of the Quality Assurance

Functionality Testing
Making sure the app or web application’s required set of features are working as expected. This stage includes extensive compatibility review.
Performance Testing
Modeling, measuring and improving overall performance and activity of particular elements of the application to make sure there are no lags in operation.
Usability Testing
Usability testing includes the examination of overall user experience and interface logistics to make sure that the user would understand how to navigate it.
Maintainability Testing
Includes monitoring of overall testability, changeability of the functional elements without issues and stability upon making changes.
